Welcome to The Indian Classical Music & Dance Festival

Join us for a mesmerizing celebration of India's rich cultural heritage through music and dance. Our annual festival brings together the finest artists from across the country, offering a unique opportunity to experience the depth and beauty of Indian classical arts.

Dates: October 15-17, 2024

Venue: Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts, New Delhi
